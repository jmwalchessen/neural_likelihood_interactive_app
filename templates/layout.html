Neural Likelihood for Spatial Processes with Intractable or Computationally Intensive Likelihoods

arxiv link

This is a website containing an interactive app for the neural likelihood of a Gaussian Process. To access the app, select GP in the menu bar. Note that length scale and variance must be between 0 and 2 and have no more than two digits after the decimal.
